a picture book by Sanchita Jain
A book for all ages — full of true stories and a bit of imagination. Filled with love, companionship, and a dash of play. For anyone who thinks cats are grumpy — come meet Ollie!
Ollie is a gentle and loving, albeit curious cat. He loves to explore, but he loves tuna more!
A short and cozy read, it’s a perfect gift for children, art lovers, cat lovers...and those who don’t know they are cat lovers (yet).
At its heart, this project is about the feeling of ‘coming home’ — told through the story of a family made whole by the love of a pet.
