a picture book by Sanchita Jain
A book for all ages — full of true stories and a bit of imagination. Filled with love, companionship, and a dash of play. For anyone who thinks cats are grumpy — come meet Ollie!
Ollie is a gentle and loving, albeit curious cat. He loves to explore. But he loves tuna more. Join him on his daily wanderings!
A short and cozy read, it’s a perfect gift for your nieces and nephews, art lovers, cat lovers...and those who don’t know they are cat lovers, yet.
